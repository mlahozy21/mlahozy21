# Marcos Lahoz Yago

**AI researcher / PhD applicant.** M2 in Mathematics & Artificial Intelligence at
Université Paris-Saclay. I work at the intersection of machine learning, deep learning
and statistical inference — from NLP and graph neural networks to Bayesian methods and
time-series forecasting — with a focus on approaches that are both principled and
reproducible.

## Selected projects

- **[Bayesian Learning of Neural Interactions](https://github.com/mlahozy21/Bayesian-Learning-of-Neural-Interactions)** — Bayesian filtering that recovers the interaction structure of a network of neurons from their spike times: exact posterior, consistency with a CLT convergence rate, and an SMC extension (theory + paper + reproducible code).
- **[Medical RAG with Knowledge Graphs](https://github.com/mlahozy21/Medical-RAG-Knowledge-Graphs)** — Multimodal medical Retrieval-Augmented Generation combining ColPali visual retrieval, Google Gemini and a Mondo Knowledge Graph; evaluated on the MIRAGE benchmark.
- **[Structural Probes for Spanish Syntax](https://github.com/mlahozy21/Structural-Probes-for-Spanish-Syntax)** — Structural probes on multilingual BERT for Spanish dependency syntax (UD AnCora), with an orthogonal/isometric-probe extension and a layer-wise analysis.
- **[Graph Convolutional Networks (from scratch)](https://github.com/mlahozy21/Graph-Convolutional-Networks-GCN)** — Reproduction of Kipf & Welling (2017): GCN vs. MLP baseline and over-smoothing experiments on Cora/Citeseer/Pubmed.
- **[Electricity Demand Forecasting — Deep Learning](https://github.com/mlahozy21/Deep-Learning-Electricity-Demand-Forecasting)** — Multi-output neural forecasting of half-hourly electricity demand across France's regions from weather and calendar features.
- **[Electricity Demand Forecasting — Statistical Models](https://github.com/mlahozy21/Electricity-Demand-Forecasting-Statistical-Models)** — Net-demand forecasting in R: GAM/Kalman, quantile GAMs and online expert aggregation (pinball loss).
- **[Predicting Hotel Cancellations & Spotify Popularity](https://github.com/mlahozy21/Predicting-Hotel-Cancellations-and-Spotify-Popularity)** — Stacked ensembles (LightGBM/CatBoost) for two supervised-learning competitions (classification & regression).
